# AppraiseAI — Design Brainstorm

## Context
AppraiseAI offers instant AI-powered property appraisals and nationwide property tax appeals filed via power of attorney or pro se. The brand needs to convey trust, authority, speed, and financial empowerment — the feeling that a powerful legal/financial ally is in your corner.

---

<response>
<idea>
**Design Movement:** Brutalist-Financial — raw authority meets precision data
**Core Principles:**
- Unapologetic bold typography as the primary visual element
- High-contrast black/white with a single electric accent (acid green #B8FF57)
- Data density as a design feature — numbers and stats are visual heroes
- Asymmetric layouts with deliberate tension and weight

**Color Philosophy:** Black (#0A0A0A) base with off-white (#F5F2ED) text panels and acid green (#B8FF57) as the sole accent. The green evokes money, growth, and digital precision. No gradients — flat, decisive.

**Layout Paradigm:** Offset grid — sections break the baseline, text blocks overlap imagery, large numerals anchor compositions. Headers sit flush-left at massive scale.

**Signature Elements:**
- Oversized dollar/percentage numerals as decorative background elements
- Thick horizontal rule dividers in acid green
- Monospace data readouts for stats and figures

**Interaction Philosophy:** Snap-to-grid hover states, instant color inversions on hover (black becomes white, white becomes black), no easing — everything is immediate.

**Animation:** Zero-delay entrance animations, text that "types in" character by character, counters that count up aggressively fast.

**Typography System:** Clash Display (bold, 900 weight) for headlines + IBM Plex Mono for data/stats + Inter for body copy.

**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric two-column layouts with a dominant left-aligned text column and a floating visual/data panel on the right. Sections use diagonal clip-paths to create visual momentum downward.

**Signature Elements:**
- Thin gold horizontal rules beneath section headings
- Frosted glass cards with subtle navy borders for feature callouts
- Document/seal iconography that references legal authority

**Interaction Philosophy:** Smooth, deliberate transitions (300ms ease-out). Hover states reveal depth — cards lift with shadow, buttons fill with gold. The site feels like turning pages of a premium document.

**Animation:** Fade-up entrance animations staggered by 100ms. Parallax on hero section. Number counters with smooth easing for stats.

**Typography System:** Playfair Display (serif, 700) for display headlines + DM Sans (400/600) for body and UI + DM Mono for data figures.

## Selected Approach: **Refined Legal-Tech**

Deep navy authority + warm cream trust + gold precision. This design philosophy positions AppraiseAI as the premium, trustworthy alternative to expensive attorneys — backed by AI speed and legal expertise. The serif/sans typography pairing communicates both gravitas and modernity.
